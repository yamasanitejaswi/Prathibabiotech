Cocoly

In the field of Agriculture, Cocoly is the world's most sophisticated Granular Water soluble Fertilizer. There is a story behind the launch of Cocoly. The scientist who invented Cocoly, during his childhood his father got ill and the whole agriculture burden was to be beared by his grand mother, then he decided to help his grand mother in farming. After a lot of struggle, they could not get enough yield. The Scientist tried various fertilizers, but could not succeed. The Chinese eat snails. One day, he found the snails he stored in the beakers dead. He dug the soil and put the dead snails and covered it with soil and planted a sapling on it. After a week, he saw all his backyard plants were pale and dull, whilst one plant has grown looking healthy. It started flowering also. He recalled that it is the place where he placed the dead snails. Immediately, he brought some more snails, killed them and put them under the plants. After a week he found that all the plants have become healthy. He did the same experiment in the field and found that the plants have grown healthy and gave more yield. Later on, the Scientist understood that there is something in the snails which benefits the plants. He started to discover the same. The result of that experiment is PAS (Polymetric Acidic Substance). The powerful PAS is present in the snails naturally. This neutralizes the pH level of the soil. In Laboratories or in production, we use one kind of chemicals to bring the high pH level to Ph7. Similarly, to bring low pH to pH7 we use another kind of chemicals. But, the PAS in the Snails got a unique property which neutralizes the pH level whether it is high or low and brings it to pH7. After the discovery of PAS, Cocoly has been designed. This PAS will be in liquid form. To change the soil's pH nature, this has to be used in the soil. Hence, it was decided to experiment to give this PAS as 100% water soluble Granulated Fertilizer form. The PAS is prepared with a mix of Nitrogen 15%, Phosphorous 5%, Pottasium 10%, Polymeric Acidic Substance 3%, Potassium Fulvic 5%, Molasses Fermentation Solubles (CMS) 2.5%, Poly Glutamic Acid 2.5%, Poly Aspartic Acid 1%, Trace Elements 0.5%, Bio root Promoter 0.3%, Bacillus Cirrus, Bacillus Sub stills bacteria and organic growth promoters to develop the root system and it is the first of its kind Granulated Water Soluble Fertilizer in the world. It is Cocoly. This has been discovered by the Chinese Company Shandong Cocoly Fertilizer Company Limited. To provide this to our Farming Community. Our Prathibha Biotech has entered into exclusive sales agreement with the Chinese Company. If every dealer and every farmer utilizes this opportunity not only will our agriculture become profitable but we can protect our soil. Cocoly is a powerful sophisticated Granulated Water Soluble Fertilizer. Cocoly can be used for all types of crops as spray, as soil application and in drip farming. Presently, Cocoly is being used by the farmers of China, Egypt, Japan and Pakistan. Their yield has increased by 25% to 30%. Let us use Cocoly. Let us bring the revolutionary change in the agriculture.

Gama

Latest combination NPK water soluble Fertilizer has been discovered after experimenting and examining the required quantity of Nitrogen, Phosphorous and Potassium to the plants during their development stage. It is Gama. Gama Water Soluble Fertilizer has Nitrogen 24%, Phosphorous 24% and Potassium 16%. If Gama is used during the plant developing stage, it enhances the growth and gives timely and high yields. Gama can be used as Spray and Drip for all crops.


Gama Dosage/h3

Composition • Nitrogen, Phosphorous, Potassium

To provide sufficient Nitrogen, Phosphorous and Potassium to the plants during developing stage. Dosages:

In Drip : 4 Kgs per Acre (5 times in 4 to 7 days)

In Spray : 1 Kg Gama mix in sufficient water and spray on one acre crop.
