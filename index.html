WELCOME TO PRATHIBHA BIOTECH PVT LTD

Prathibha Biotech Pvt Ltd has come a long way since it was set up by Shri E. Rajasekhar Reddy in 2005. Prathibha Biotech Pvt Ltd, which began with the vision to transform Indian agriculture by promoting innovative agri-biotech products among farmers, celebrated 10 years of its successful existence in 2015. When the company entered this field, agri-biotech was yet to find many takers in India.

However, thanks to the vision and dynamic leadership of its founder, Prathibha Biotech Pvt Ltd, today, has successfully established itself as one of the leading names in the agri-biotech in India, creating greater awareness among the farmers community about the benefits of agri-biotech products.

The company’s promoters bring with them vast experience in the manufacture of Biotechnology products, Vermiculture-, Tissue-Culture and Bio-Organic processes.
